Larissa's Exam Care Package



Hello Larissa. Glad you made it here. Allow me to firstly clarify what this is not:
  • This is not a plea
  • This is not a request
  • This is not a 'love-bomb'
What this is, is a a care package from someone you used to know. And someone you might yet get to know. A wretched sailor, rowing furiously toward your harbor.

This is a declaration of intent.

I cannot make up for the times I had the opportunity to gift you, and let the darker side of me prevent that. Nor am I trying to make up for that. I can, however, make sure I don’t regret not making full use of any more opportunities presented to me.

If it makes you feel better, you could frame it like this: I'm the dude you went on a coffee date with once, and since then he'd been a little over-obsessed, and you've been trying to get rid of him but he keeps on sending gifts that make you uncomfortable because you can see he's overcommitting.
Shame.

Included in your care package is a little something for every possible craving I know you might have.
You'll also find a small bottle of champagne. This is to be consumed strictly AFTER your last exam.
The reason I included it is because I know very well that it is unlikely I will be there to celebrate with you. So, it is my way of saying "Congratulations" without being there in person

Please pace yourself. There are many calories in this box.

Take the blue pill, and you remain blisfully unaware.
Take the red pill, and you'll be shown the truth.

I almost convinced myself not to send this. Told myself to let bygones be bygones, the past is the past for a reason, that that ship had indeed sailed and is now far away.

But what the fuck is the point of feeling a feeling if you don't express it? In the same way, what is the point of life if you don't have someone to share it with?

So I do this without expectation. I just know I must. There is no risk, because I have nothing to lose, only someone to gain.

I absolutely do not want us to 'get back together' or try to fix what we had. But best believe I plan on pursuing you, all over again. And this time, my house won't be built on sand.

Lastly, I have a question for you:
If all your work is under control, you wanna grab a coffee sometime?
